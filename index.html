The Living Dead

Revealing Social Connection over the Centuries in Obituaries

A project analyzing how obituaries across centuries reveal changing patterns of connection, inequality, and remembrance.

Time & Place

Focused on English-language obituaries from Pennsylvania and Georgia, from the 18th century through the present, with particular attention to discrepancies across race, social status, and gender.

Approach

A mixed-methods project combining close reading and qualitative coding with computational text analysis, social network approaches, and large language models to trace patterns of social connection and inequality over time.

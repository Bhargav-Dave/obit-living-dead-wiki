Notes here focus on the large, digitized newspaper collections that complement Legacy.com and targeted obituary sources.

Sources Used

The general newspapers corpus currently draws on the following collections, with room to expand as additional archives become available:

  • Chronicling America
  • Georgia Historic Newspapers
  • Pennsylvania Newspaper Archive
  • Open to other library and funeral home sources

Metadata

A finer dissection of the Georgia and Pennsylvania state archive metadata was done by Eunkyung and is documented in this state archive data collection plan.

Even more detailed metadata (for example, frequency of publication and related descriptive variables) was scraped by Bhargav and is present in Excel sheets in the relevant Teams folder.

Text Scraping Methodology

We scrape selected newspaper pages from a subset of focal titles in each archive. Candidate pages are first filtered based on the presence of obituary-related keywords and the absence of a list of excluded terms. These wordlists are maintained collaboratively in a shared Google document and are updated as we refine our understanding of obituary language and archive-specific noise.

For more information on how this data is processed, visit the data processing page.

General newspapers

This page documents our collection of obituary pages from historic and contemporary newspapers in Georgia and Pennsylvania. These sources provide long-run local coverage and contextual reporting around deaths, funerals, and community life.

Sources

Key newspaper and archive platforms currently in scope:

  • Chronicling America – https://www.loc.gov/collections/chronicling-america/
  • Georgia Historic Newspapers – https://gahistoricnewspapers.galileo.usg.edu/
  • Pennsylvania Newspaper Archive – https://panewsarchive.psu.edu/newspapers/
  • Other library and funeral-home sources – additional regional and institutional newspaper archives may be added as coverage and access conditions become clearer.

Metadata

A finer dissection of Georgia and Pennsylvania state-archive newspaper metadata was prepared by Eunkyung and is available here: state-archive-data-collection-plan.html .

Additional structured metadata (such as frequency of publication and other title-level descriptors) was scraped by Bhargav and is stored as Excel workbooks in the relevant Teams folder.

Text scraping methodology

We scrape selected pages from a subset of newspapers identified as especially relevant for obituary coverage. Within those titles, pages are filtered based on the presence of obituary-related terms and the absence of excluded words that typically signal non-obituary content.

The inclusion and exclusion wordlists are maintained collaboratively in a shared Google document and are being iteratively refined as we inspect false positives and false negatives. Finalized lists and implemented filters will be documented alongside code in the internal repository.

For more information on how this data is processed, see the Data Processing page.

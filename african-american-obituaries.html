Overview

This page is for tracking African American obituary material collected in a targeted way, beyond what appears in large aggregators. The emphasis is on sources where African American communities, institutions, and families are centered in the records.

We can use this space to keep track of which collections we have identified, which ones we have scraped, and how these materials complement coverage from mainstream newspaper sources.

Source List

Initial examples (to be fleshed out as the project proceeds):

— BlackCemeteries.com and similar cemetery documentation projects.
— Funeral home obituary collections with strong African American client bases.
— Special collections in university and public libraries focusing on Black life in Pennsylvania and Georgia.
— Black newspapers not fully covered by major digitization portals.

As we confirm specific collections and links, we can list them here with brief notes on coverage and format.

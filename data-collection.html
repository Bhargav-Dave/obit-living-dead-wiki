Data Collection

The data collection module coordinates how we acquire obituary texts and related metadata from a variety of sources, including digitized newspapers, online archives, and funeral program collections. The goal is to assemble a transparent, reproducible corpus that can support both qualitative and computational analysis.

Source Pages

Use these cards to jump into working notes for each major data source. Each page can track scraping logic, filters, and open questions. Fully collected datasets have a greenish tint.

Legacy.com

Commercial obituary platform with broad coverage from 2000 onward. Tracks coverage, regional focus, and internal completeness checks.

African American Obituaries

Notes on targeted collection strategies for African American obituaries across newspapers, community archives, and specialized databases.

General Newspapers

Coverage plans and extraction notes for state archives, Chronicling America, and other general-interest newspaper collections.

Celebrity Obituaries

Methods for assembling a cross-national set of celebrity deaths from Wikipedia, WikiTree, and national press, with attention to selection bias.

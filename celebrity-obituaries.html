Overview

The celebrity obituary set provides a complementary perspective to local and regional obituaries. It focuses on nationally visible figures whose deaths are covered across multiple outlets and platforms.

This page can document how we define “celebrity” for the project, how we build the list of individuals, and how we link their obituaries back to structured biographical data.

Construction

Potential construction strategy (TBA):

— Start from curated lists (e.g., Wikipedia “deaths in year” pages, occupation-specific lists).
— Apply filters for connection to Pennsylvania and Georgia where appropriate (birth, residence, or primary career location).
— Record which outlets we use for the obituary texts themselves (e.g., national newspapers, Legacy.com, major regional papers).

Once finalized, we can summarize sample sizes, temporal coverage, and any biases in the selection process.

Overview

Legacy.com hosts obituary content from a wide range of partner newspapers and funeral homes. For this project, we treat Legacy.com as a large, heterogeneous aggregator that needs clear filters to isolate Pennsylvania- and Georgia-focused content.

This page can document which subdomains, partner newspapers, and date ranges we include, along with any constraints imposed by search interfaces or terms of use.

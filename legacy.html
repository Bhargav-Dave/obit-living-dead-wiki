Methodology

For Legacy.com, we collected obituaries for the states of Georgia and Pennsylvania across the years 2000 to 2024 (both inclusive). For each year in each state, we randomly selected two months that are approximately six months apart (for example, April and October). Within each selected month, we then gathered the first 1,000 obituaries that appeared after applying the relevant filters.

This design provides a regular temporal sampling frame across a 25-year window while keeping scraping volume tractable and making it easier to compare patterns across states and years.

Step 1

Go to Legacy.com and access the obituary search interface.

Step 2

Filter by state (Georgia or Pennsylvania).

Step 3

Choose a specific year between 2000 and 2024.

Step 4

For that state–year combination, randomly select two months that are roughly six months apart (e.g., April and October).

Step 5

For each selected month, apply the month filter and retrieve the first 1,000 obituaries returned by the search.

Step 6

Store obituary text and key metadata (dates, locations, and basic identifiers) in the project’s standardized schema.

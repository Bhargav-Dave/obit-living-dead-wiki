Methodology

Collected obituaries for the states of Georgia and Pennsylvania for the years 2000 to 2024 (inclusive). For each year in each state, we randomly selected two months that are approximately six months apart (for example, April and October). Within each chosen month, we scraped the first 1,000 obituaries returned after applying the appropriate filters.
